Welcome to my website.

I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Iowa State University, advised by Prof. Namrata Vaswani.

My research interests include matrix factorization, machine learning, signal processing, data privacy, low-rank recovery, and robust data analysis.

Before joining Iowa State University, I completed my B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), where I worked on my undergraduate thesis under the supervision of Prof. Hafiz Imtiaz.

This website contains information about my research, publications, projects, teaching, and academic background.
